Emrys Brandt is a trans-disciplinary artist and researcher based in Chicago, IL, on the stolen land of the Ojibwe, Potawatomi, and Odawa peoples.

notable current projects include:
Seasonal Coordinator at Bird Show, an outdoor exhibition space in Chicago.
Co-Director of Craft Nite, a queer event space in Ukranian Village, Chicago.
Co-Host of Medievalism Today, a podcast about the Middle Ages, Technology, and Contemporary Aesthetics.
